# CHARACTERS

Quick-reference for every character in Gipfelbuch.
When adding, changing or removing a character, update this file.

---

## Lukas (Player)

**Background**
Rosa Oberhofer's grandson. Arrives at Campingplatz Gamsblick on a Friday night with a tent, no phone signal, and Oma Rosa's 1974 hiking journal. Slowly retraces the route Rosa and her friend Ida pioneered as the first women over the Gamswand north edge.

**Character**
Quiet, curious, a little overwhelmed at first. Grows from cautious camper into confident mountaineer over one weekend. Gentle humour; never speaks much — the mountain does the talking.

**Visual description**
Young hiker, early 20s. Pale cream skin tone (#f0d2ab) with two dark oval eyes (#2a2724), a rosy cheek, and light blonde hair (#d8b673) peeking out from under a golden-mustard knit beanie (#e3ad44) with a ribbed dome, rolled brim (#d29a34), and a small folded knob on top. A rounded knit sweater/jacket: teal-green (#2e7d6b) by default, upgrading to a brick-red rain jacket (#c14d35) mid-game, with a darker round neckline and a soft front highlight. Denim-blue trousers (#3d5f8a) with baggy cuffs and seam lines. Olive-green backpack (#647a4a) with a darker lid (#54683c), a front pocket panel, compression strap, and a signature orange ring buckle (#e89441); an olive shoulder strap and orange sternum-strap buckle show on the chest. Grey sneakers at the start, switching to chunky brown hiking boots (#8a5530) with a lighter tan upper cuff (#aa794c) and dark soles (#45291a). Headlamp (#4a4e55) mounts on the beanie when acquired. When gliding, a red-and-cream paraglider canopy arcs overhead.

## Greta

**Background**
A regular at the campsite, old enough to remember Rosa's stories firsthand. Always the first one up, always by the fire. Owns Strolch the dog.

**Character**
Warm but direct — she'll pour you coffee and tell you you're under-equipped in the same breath. Knows the mountain and its people like the back of her hand. A quiet keeper of local memory.

**Visual description**
Older woman, roughly 60s–70s. Warm skin (#e8b88a). Silver-grey hair pulled into a small bun on top of her head (#cfcfcf). A muted plum-purple blouse or cardigan (#6f5a7d). Dark brown-black trousers and sturdy dark shoes (#3d3327). Sturdy, grounded stance. No hat, no accessories — she dresses practically and has been doing so for decades.

## Strolch

**Background**
Greta's dog. Self-appointed night-watch of the entire campsite. Works unpaid.

**Character**
Loyal, enthusiastic, slightly too friendly. Barks first, wags second. Greta has to call him off from smothering visitors.

**Visual description**
Medium-sized mutt, warm tawny-brown fur (#8a6a44). Blocky rectangular body with a rounded head. One pointed ear that sticks up. Short legs, stubby tail that wags in a sine-wave arc. Small, expressive — roughly half the height of a human NPC.

## Norbert

**Background**
Hut keeper of the Gamsblick-Alm (est. 1924). Son of Toni, the young man who gave Rosa and Ida Knödel in 1974 and never laughed at their plan. Norbert inherited the hut — and the story.

**Character**
Gruff, generous, old-school Tyrolean hospitality. Trades chestnuts for Knödel. Recognises Rosa's journal instantly and goes quiet for a moment before handing over the rain jacket she left behind half a century ago.

**Visual description**
Stocky man, middle-aged. Warm skin (#e8b88a). Dark moss-green shirt or loden jacket (#3f5e3a), a navy-blue work apron tied over it (#2b3a66). Classic Tyrolean hat — olive-green felt (#4a5e3a) with a tiny pink-red feather (#d9577a) tucked in the band. Dark brown-black trousers and sturdy boots (#3d3327). Solid, square build — he looks like he could carry a crate of Knödel up the scree path without breaking stride.

## Vera

**Background**
Runs the one-woman Flugschule Gamstal (flight school) in the hidden valley beyond the ridge sub-summit. The player reaches her by climbing down the cable from the slip behind the old summit. Vera grants the paraglider on first meeting — the mountain taught you to walk, now she teaches you to fly. Saw Rosa's paraglider over the Gamstal as a child and never forgot it.

**Character**
Confident, wry, a little wild. Lives at the edge of the map where the mountain "isn't finished yet." Grants the glider as a mid-game unlock, challenges the player to fly five rings, and awards a tongue-in-cheek diploma. Underneath the bravado she clearly loved Rosa — her voice catches for half a sentence before she covers it up.

**Visual description**
Athletic woman, late 20s–30s. Warm skin (#e8b88a). Deep rust-red flight suit or jumpsuit (#b8483a). White half-dome flight helmet covering the top of her head (#fff). Aviator-style dark sunglasses — a single horizontal line across the eyes (#888). Dark brown-black boots (#3d3327). Lean, upright posture — she looks like she could step off a cliff and enjoy it.

## Die Gams (The Chamois)

**Background**
A lone female chamois that appears near the player's next objective throughout the game. Not a pet, not a guide — she simply happens to be wherever you're going next. After the finale she lies down at the summit and watches you calmly.

**Character**
Skittish and proud. Bounds away if you approach too fast, then reappears further ahead as if she's always been there. At the very end, she stops running. You've both earned the rest.

**Visual description**
Compact, angular body in warm umber-brown (#8a6f50). Rectangular torso, thin straight legs, a narrow neck that rises into a small round head. Two short hooked horns drawn in dark charcoal (#3d3327), curving back. A single dark eye-stripe across the face (#2c2a25). When resting, her body flattens into a low ellipse and the horns tilt sideways.

## Die Kuh (The Cow)

**Background**
Grazes the Gamsblick-Alm pasture. Drifts slowly back and forth, unbothered by everything.

**Character**
Unimpressed. Flatly "Muh." She is the only creature on the mountain truly indifferent to your quest.

**Visual description**
Large, boxy body in warm tawny-brown (#9a6f4a) with a cream-white patch on the flank (#e8e0cd). Small rounded head with two stubby off-white horns (#d8d2bd). Three dark legs visible (#3d3327). A small brass cowbell (#c9b46a) hangs under the chin. Wider and heavier than any other character.

## Die Murmeltiere (The Marmots — ×5)

**Background**
Five marmots scattered across the mountain, each in its own burrow. Spotting all five is a side-quest; once complete, they whistle and wave whenever you pass.

**Character**
Watchful, cheeky, communal. They stand bolt-upright when alerted, dive into their burrows when startled, and eventually come out to greet you with a tiny raised paw.

**Visual description**
Small, plump rodents in sandy-gold fur (#b08a55) with a pale cream belly (#d8c9a8). Round head atop a rectangular body, sitting upright on haunches. One tiny dark eye (#2c2a25). Each burrow is a small brown ellipse in the ground (#5a4a35). When greeting, one paw extends sideways from the body.
